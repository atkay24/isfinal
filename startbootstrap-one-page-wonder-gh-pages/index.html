Animal Crossing Villagers

MY TOP THREE

Bubbles

Bubbles (チャコ, Chako?) is a peppy hippo villager in the Animal Crossing series. She has appeared in the GCN games and more recently in New Leaf and New Horizons. Bubbles' name may come from hippos' behavior of blowing bubbles, her bubbly personality, or Bubbles from The Powerpuff Girls. Her initial phrase could be a reference to both her species and to people who "have done things before they were cool". She has the fashion hobby.

Apollo

Apollo (アポロ, Aporo?, Apollo) is a cranky eagle villager in the Animal Crossing series. He also appears in Gekijōban Dōbutsu no Mori as a minor character. Apollo is a bald eagle, specifically, the national bird of the United States of America, hence his July 4th birthday (Independence Day in the United States). He has the music hobby.

Alice

Alice (メルボルン, Meruborun?, Melbourne) is a normal koala villager in the Animal Crossing series. Her name is based on the town of Alice Springs, Australia. Her Japanese and Korean name comes from Melbourne, the state capital of Victoria, Australia. She originally appeared in the Japanese only Animal Forest e+ as an exclusive character, and was brought back to Wild World, City Folk, and New Leaf. She appears on the album cover for K.K. Stroll. She has the education hobby.
